About

At TSA, we offer holistic, end-to-end solutions tailored to align with your business goals and financial objectives.

Whether you are a startup, a growing enterprise, or an established organization, we provide strategic support across every stage of your journey — from compliance and taxation to business advisory, virtual CFO services, and operational planning.

TSA is your one-stop destination for comprehensive financial and business services, delivered by a professional team including Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Cost Accountants, Advocates, and MBAs.

We collaborate to provide audit, consulting, financial advisory, and tax services.

Our Approach And Principles

At T SURENDRA & ASSOCIATES, we combine analytical precision with a practical, hands-on methodology tailored to each client’s unique needs. Our approach is built on trust, clarity, and long-term partnership.

  • Maintaining confidentiality and professional ethics
  • Continuously upgrading our skills and technologies
